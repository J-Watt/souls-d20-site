Frozen Warriors

The Frozen Warriors Festival (one-shot session)

The city of Olafis sits in an arid land of tall mesas and rock spires. As a gateway between the desert empire of Erume and the rest of the continent of Archea, Olafis has long been a center of trade and travel. To celebrate this, a warriors festival is held: outsiders test their strength and styles against the best of the city.

But Olafis now teeters on ruin. The frost dragon Acire, with thousands of northern soldiers, swept in and seized the city. Twisted into a symbol of oppression, the festival now disposes of prisoners and dissidents. You and your group are trapped in this torturous colosseum, seeking freedom or revenge.
